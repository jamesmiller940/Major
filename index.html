Methodological Diagnostic

The Architecture of Inquiry

Choosing a major is often framed as a vocational pipeline. However, at Duke Kunshan, we view a major as a mode of inquiry—a specialized lens through which you learn to decompose complexity and analyze evidence.

This tool identifies the underlying intellectual architecture of your mind. It does not measure what you want to "be," but rather how you naturally prefer to solve an unsolved problem.

You will be presented with 15 randomized research scenarios. For each, identify the methodological approach that resonates most deeply with your curiosity.

Method over Major

Your results represent your preferred unit of analysis. Careers are built on how you think; masters of a specific methodology are more prepared for a global landscape than those who have merely memorized facts.
